About Me

I was born in DC, grew up in MD. From a young age, I was climbing everything that I could (as well as many things that I shouldn't). Into adulthood, this translated into becoming a rock climber where people don't get so mad at you for climbing things.

I love learning working and school was a perfect outlet for that. I graduated from UMD a semester early -- not because I was especially smart, but because I loved the challenge.

I'm an active memeber at Washington Improv Theater (or WIT) as both an improviser and a volunteer. Although it has only been a year, improv has had a huge impact on my life. Just in being able to talk to people it has helped, but also at work when I need to support someone, I am now considerably equipped to do so.

I like to make things and help people.
